## Beta 3

Beta 3 is now available with the [latest features and changes](/about/versions/17/blog-beta3) to try with your apps. To get started, [get the beta](/about/versions/17/get) and [update your tools](/about/versions/17/setup-sdk). Android 17 Beta is available to users as well as developers, so you should test your app's compatibility and release any needed updates. Try the new features and share your feedback [through our issue tracker](/about/versions/17/feedback#issue_tracker).
