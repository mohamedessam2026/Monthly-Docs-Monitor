### [Android 16 QPR2 Beta](https://developer.android.com/about/versions/16/qpr2/get)

The Android 16 QPR2 Beta is now available for development, testing, and feedback! Enroll your device to try it today on Pixel.

[Get started](https://developer.android.com/about/versions/16/qpr2/get)
[Test your app](https://developer.android.com/about/versions/16/qpr2/get#on_emulator)

Android 16 QPR2 Beta 3 is now available for you to try with your apps. To get started, [get the beta](/about/versions/16/qpr2/get) and [update your tools](/about/versions/16/qpr2/setup-sdk). Android 16 Beta is available to users as well as developers, so you should test your app's compatibility and release any needed updates.

## Get started with Android QPR2

1. **Set up a runtime environment** — see [Get
   Android 16 QPR2](/about/versions/16/qpr2/get) to flash a Google Pixel device or set up an emulator.
2. **Set up Android Studio** — try the Android 16 SDK and tools.
   See the [SDK setup](/about/versions/16/qpr2/setup-sdk) page for steps.
